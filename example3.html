Example 3: Gaussian Emission lines - correlations¶

In :doc:example2, we analysed emission lines. We now have the derived parameters distribution for each spectrum.

  • Are the line widths and heights correlated?
  • Are the line heights and positions correlated?
  • If yes, what are the parameters of this correlations?

How would you answer these questions?

Keep in mind, that you want to take into account the uncertainties in the derived parameters!
